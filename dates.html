Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the competition timeline and deadlines.

What happens if I miss the abstract submission deadline?

Late submissions are generally not accepted. However, in exceptional cases with valid documentation, a short extension may be granted at the discretion of the organizing committee.

Can I update my abstract after submission?

Minor updates may be permitted until the abstract submission deadline. After the review process begins, no changes will be allowed.

How will I receive the acceptance notification?

All notifications will be sent via email to the corresponding author. Please ensure that emails from our domain are not filtered as spam.

Is physical attendance mandatory for all competition days?

Yes, at least one author must be physically present during the poster presentation and judging sessions. Virtual attendance options are available only under special circumstances.

What if I cannot attend the final days due to unforeseen circumstances?

Please contact the organizing committee immediately. In cases of emergency, you may nominate a co-author to present, or in exceptional cases, alternative arrangements may be made.
