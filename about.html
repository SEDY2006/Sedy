About the Competition

Learn more about our prestigious research poster competition, our mission, and what makes it special.

Fostering Innovation Through Visual Research

The Research Poster Competition at Techno International New Town (TINT) was established in 2018 with the vision to provide a platform for researchers to showcase their work in a visually compelling format.

Now in its 7th year, our competition has grown to become one of the most prestigious research events in the region, attracting participants from across India and neighboring countries.

We believe that effective visual communication of research is just as important as the research itself. Our competition encourages researchers to present complex findings in accessible, engaging formats that can reach both expert and non-expert audiences.
