Location & Directions

The Research Poster Competition 2025 will be held at Techno International New Town (TINT) campus in Kolkata.

How to Reach

From Airport

Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose International Airport is approximately 7 km from the venue. Taxis and app-based cabs are readily available.

From Railway Station

Sealdah and Howrah Railway Stations are approximately 15 km away. Take a taxi or metro to New Town.

Public Transport

Bus routes 30B, 47B, and AC-20 stop near the campus. The nearest metro station is New Town, a short auto-rickshaw ride away.

Parking

Limited free parking is available on campus for registered participants. Please display your registration confirmation on your dashboard.
